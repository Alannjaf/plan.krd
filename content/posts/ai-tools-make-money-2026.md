
## Avoiding Common Mistakes

### Mistake 1: "AI will do everything"
**Reality:** AI does 60-70% of work. You add the 30% that makes it valuable (insight, context, quality control).

### Mistake 2: "Clients will know it's AI"
**Truth:** If you edit and add value, the output is yours. But be transparent when asked.

### Mistake 3: "Too expensive"
**Reality:** One client project pays for months of subscriptions. It's an investment, not an expense.

### Mistake 4: "I need all the tools"
**Truth:** Start with 1-2 tools, master them, then expand.
